Foxglove Films

Foxglove Films loves working with up-and-coming directors, helping them hone and shape their unique vision. Recent credits include Adam Meeks' Union County, starring Will Poulter and Noah Centineo, which premiered in the U.S. Dramatic Competition at the 2026 Sundance Film Festival.

Previous credits include Theda Hammel's Stress Positions, starring John Early, which premiered in Dramatic Competition at the 2024 Sundance Film Festival, played New Directors/New Films Closing Night (2024), and was distributed by NEON, and Simon Hacker's directorial debut Notice to Quit (2024, MUBI), starring Michael Zegen. Forthcoming projects include features from filmmakers Sophia Takal and Stephen Cone.

Prior to Foxglove, Stephanie produced the Emmy-nominated Netflix Original documentary series Principles of Pleasure, which premiered on Netflix in 2022. She won a James Beard Media Award for her documentary work with chef Vivian Howard in 2018. Her writing has been featured in The New York Times, Filmmaker Mag, and VICE, among other publications.

She was a 2024 Gotham / Cannes Producers Network Fellow. She grew up in the Pacific Northwest, attended Middlebury College, and lives in Brooklyn with her husband and their two cats, Ziggy and Finn.
