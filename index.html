About

Stephanie Roush is an independent producer and founder of Foxglove Films. Recent credits include Adam Meeks' Union County, starring Will Poulter and Noah Centineo, which premiered in the U.S. Dramatic Competition at the 2026 Sundance Film Festival. Previous credits include Theda Hammel's Stress Positions, starring John Early, which premiered in Dramatic Competition at the 2024 Sundance Film Festival, played New Directors/New Films Closing Night (2024), and was distributed by NEON, and Simon Hacker's directorial debut Notice to Quit (2024, Mubi), starring Michael Zegen. Forthcoming projects include features from filmmakers Sophia Takal and Stephen Cone.

Previously, Stephanie developed and co-produced the Emmy-nominated Netflix Original documentary series Principles of Pleasure which premiered on Netflix in 2022. She won a James Beard Media Award for her documentary work with chef Vivian Howard in 2018. She was a 2024 Gotham / Cannes Producers Network Fellow.
